Vigilante Privacy notes

Based on our review of the privacy policies and settings at Character.AI (https://character.ai/), we recommend the following best practices to protect your data.

Documents:

Character.AI does not prompt you to accept their Terms of Service. You can review their documentation here:

Terms of Service: https://character.ai/tos
Privacy Policy: https://character.ai/privacy
Privacy Choices: https://character.ai/privacy-choices

These documents were last updated on October 25, 2023.

For any concerns regarding your data privacy, or to exercise your data privacy rights based on the regulations in your local area, please use the following contact information provided.
Contact: https://support.character.ai/hc/en-us/requests/new

Authentication:

You may only log in to Character.AI using an email or by linking your Google or Apple accounts, and your email may be shared with analytics services, marketing partners, and other third parties. Consider using a separate email address from your official, work, or school accounts. Make sure your email account is appropriately secured as well.

Tracking:

Character.AI presumes you accept the use of cookies and web tracking (cookies, pixels, beacons, etc.), and it does not provide a way to manage these settings within the tool. Consider using a private browsing session or extensions like ad blockers that may monitor and prevent web tracking.

Character.AI uses Google Analytics. You can opt-out by installing a browser extension provided by Google.

Character.AI may track your general location. We recommend reviewing your browser settings to deny permission to your location. Consider using a VPN to mask your IP address.

Content:

Character.AI states that the content you generate may be used to train their language models. Please avoid inputting any sensitive information when using the site.

Content you create on Character.AI has visibility settings. Consider using Private to protect any inference from the content you create back to your account.

Character.AI hides your likes and interactions, but which accounts you follow are always visible.

Character.AI does not apply differential privacy to view and like counts. Content with high popularity (threshold not specified) may be retained even if you request deletion of your data.

User Rights:

Users may exercise their legal privacy rights on Character.AI by submitting a ticket here: https://support.character.ai/hc/en-us/requests/new

While this ticket system does not require authentication, please remember to not provide any sensitive information beyond what is legally necessary.

GDPR: The privacy policy references the European Union's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). While it does not outline all your rights, you may legally have the right to request a copy of your data, request corrections of your data, and request deletion of your data.

Nevada: You may submit a "Nevada Do Not Sell Request" to protect your data from being sold.

California: The privacy policy references the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Their privacy policy outlines and categorizes the personal information they collect, as well as their retention policies. Their service does not respond to browser-initiated Do Not Track signals.

COPPA: The privacy policy references the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). Character.AI will delete any personal information discovered for users under the age of 13 in America or 16 in Europe. It may retain some data where permitted.
